COMMON QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do results typically take?

+
Most clients see noticeable changes within 4-6 weeks. Significant transformations typically occur within 12-16 weeks. Results depend on consistency, starting point, and adherence to the system. I provide weekly check-ins and adjustments to ensure you're progressing.

Do I need gym experience?

+
No! I work with complete beginners to advanced athletes. Every program is scaled to your current fitness level. I provide detailed form guides, video tutorials, and personalized modifications to ensure safe, effective training.

Is this online or in-person?

+
Both! I offer online coaching for busy professionals worldwide and in-person training for clients in Mombasa. Choose what works best for your lifestyle. Online coaching includes detailed plans, video check-ins, and constant support.

What if I have injuries or limitations?

+
We assess all injuries and limitations during consultation. I modify exercises and programs to work around your limitations while building strength safely. Many clients have overcome injuries through our structured approach.

What's included in the coaching package?

+
Each package includes: personalized training program, nutrition plan, mindset coaching, weekly check-ins, form video reviews, progress tracking, lifestyle support, and access to resources. Everything you need for complete transformation.

How much does coaching cost?

+
Pricing varies based on program length and intensity. I offer flexible payment plans ranging from 4-week intensive programs to 12-month transformations. Contact me for a custom quote based on your specific needs and budget.
